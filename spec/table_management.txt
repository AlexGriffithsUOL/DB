

PAGES 2 AND 3 are initially reserved for the system_tables, system_columns metadata

Example: system_tables schema
system_tables_schema = Schema([
    ('table_id', DataType.INTEGER),
    ('table_name', DataType.STRING),
    ('first_page_id', DataType.INTEGER),
])

Example: system_columns schema
system_columns_schema = Schema([
    ('table_id', DataType.INTEGER),
    ('column_name', DataType.STRING),
    ('data_type', DataType.STRING),
    ('ordinal_position', DataType.INTEGER),
])



table_id	table_name	first_page_id
1	'system_tables'	0
2	'system_columns'	1

And the system_columns entries that describe system_tables:

table_id	column_name	data_type	ordinal_position
1	'table_id'	'int'	0
1	'table_name'	'string'	1
1	'first_page_id'	'int'	2
